GUIDE QUESTIONS

1. System Functionality and Technical Depth

  • How does the system handle multiple buses moving simultaneously on overlapping routes?
  • What technologies will be used for real-time GPS integration, and how will data latency be minimized?
  • How is the Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) calculated? Are traffic conditions considered?
  • What fallback system is in place if GPS devices go offline?
  • How will the mobile app sync with the server during low connectivity?

2. User Interface and Experience

  • Can you walk us through the commuter flow—how does a passenger search, get updates, and board?
  • How is accessibility considered (e.g., multilingual support, visually impaired users)?
  • What are the top 3 usability features that make SubayBus better than existing transit apps?

3. Data and Analytics

  • What specific data points will be collected from users, buses, and terminals?
  • How will you ensure data privacy, especially for user location and activity?
  • What real-time analytics will be accessible to administrators or LGUs?

4. Deployment and Hardware Integration

  • Which models of PUVs (public utility vehicles) will be included in the initial deployment?
  • What’s the strategy for hardware installation, maintenance, and calibration of GPS devices?
  • How will the system adapt to traditional jeepneys or future EV transport models?

5. Scalability and Modular Design

  • How is the system architected for expansion into other cities?
  • Can modules like notifications, route advisory, or terminal signage be reused independently?
  • What happens if a new transport regulation requires integration with LGU databases or RFID tap cards?

6. Stakeholder Engagement and Feedback

  • What feedback mechanisms are built in for passengers and operators?
  • How were Roxas City commuters involved during ideation?
  • What incentives are there for transport operators to adopt the system?

7. Project Management and Development Strategy

  • What development methodology was used - Agile, Waterfall, or a hybrid?
  • What’s your most critical dependency before SubayBus becomes operational?
  • How did your team validate the problem statement with real-world data?
