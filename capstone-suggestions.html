Capstone Project Ideas

Organized List of Proposed Systems

Finance and Cooperative Management Systems

Small Cooperative Savings and Loan Monitoring System

Problem: Manual ledgers lead to errors and disputes in tracking savings and loans.

System Idea: Tracks member savings, loans, and payments digitally.

Novelty:

  • Automated balance computation
  • Payment history tracking
  • Transparent member accounts

Local Cooperative Member Profit Sharing System

Problem: Manual computation of profit sharing is error-prone.

System Idea: Automates computation of member profit shares.

Novelty:

  • Automated computation of shares
  • Membership-based financial tracking
  • Fair distribution system

Barangay Community Management Systems

Barangay Youth Event Registration and Participation System

Problem: Manual registration and attendance tracking in youth events.

System Idea: Manages registration, attendance, and participation records.

Novelty:

  • Event registration and attendance tracking
  • Certificate generation
  • SK activity monitoring

Barangay Pet Registration and Vaccination Monitoring System

Problem: Lack of proper pet registration and vaccination monitoring.

System Idea: Tracks pets, owners, and vaccination schedules.

Novelty:

  • Pet and owner registry
  • Vaccination reminders
  • Rabies monitoring support

Health and Safety Monitoring Systems

Barangay Animal Bite Incident and Vaccination Tracking System

Problem: Poor tracking of animal bite cases and follow-up vaccinations.

System Idea: Records bite incidents and vaccination schedules.

Novelty:

  • Incident logging system
  • Vaccination schedule tracking
  • Follow-up reminders

Public Records and Infrastructure Systems

Local Cemetery Burial Record and Plot Mapping System

Problem: Paper-based cemetery records are hard to manage and locate.

System Idea: Digitizes burial records and maps grave locations.

Novelty:

  • Digital burial database
  • Grave location indexing
  • Simple plot mapping system
