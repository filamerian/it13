Strongest Part

The proposal successfully demonstrated that the problem is not hypothetical and affects daily cooperative operations.

This is usually the most important requirement during proposal approval.

Minor Weaknesses / Needed Improvements

1. Avoid Generic "Management Information System" Title

The title is too broad.

Suggested improvement:

  • Web-Based Cooperative Management Information System for CASCOFAMCO
  • or specify major modules such as member management, production monitoring, sales, and reporting

2. Need Quantifiable Evidence

Statements are good but need measurable proof.

Add examples like:

  • Monthly reports take 2-3 days to prepare
  • Records are duplicated or misplaced
  • Staff spend several hours consolidating logbooks

Panels usually look for measurable operational impact.

3. Clarify the Existing System

Item 16 mentions Excel files, but earlier sections mention paper-based processes.

Clarify whether they use:

  • purely manual logbooks
  • Excel files
  • or both

Consistency is important during defense.

4. Define Scope Carefully

Avoid making the system too large.

Recommended modules only:

  • Member Management
  • Production Recording
  • Sales Recording
  • Task Monitoring
  • Report Generation

Avoid adding:

  • AI
  • forecasting
  • GIS
  • mobile apps
  • accounting integration unless necessary
