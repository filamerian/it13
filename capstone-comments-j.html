IOT-BASED RAIN RECYCLED WATERING SYSTEM

1. Finalize Documentation (Before Approval)

  • Refine Chapters I–III:

    • Ensure objectives are measurable and aligned with BSIT program outcomes.

    • Add evaluation plan (how success of the prototype will be measured: e.g., water savings %, uptime, usability scores).

    • Include ethical, legal, and safety considerations (RA 6716, data handling, electrical safety).

  • Prepare a Gantt Chart with clear timelines for design, implementation, testing, and evaluation.

  • Submit proposal for panel review and approval.

2. Prototype Design & Development

  • Hardware Assembly

    • Procure ESP32, soil moisture sensors, pumps, relays, rainwater tank, and battery backup.

    • Document cost estimates and budget tracking.

  • Software Development

    • Build mobile/web interface (start with simple monitoring dashboard).

    • Integrate notification/alert system (SMS, push, or app-based).

  • System Integration

    • Connect sensors → ESP32 → application interface.

    • Implement both manual and auto modes.

3. Testing & Validation (After Approval)

  • Unit Testing: Test each hardware component (sensor accuracy, pump reliability, battery performance).

  • Integration Testing: Verify sensor data flow, ESP32 decisions, and app control.

  • Performance Testing: Compare water use of IoT system vs. traditional irrigation.

  • User Testing: Conduct a small pilot with farmers or greenhouse owners for usability feedback.

4. Evaluation & Data Collection

  • Define evaluation metrics:

    • % water savings compared to manual irrigation.

    • Sensor accuracy rate (target: >90%).

    • Uptime/reliability (hours of continuous operation).

    • User satisfaction (via TAM-based survey).

  • Record quantitative and qualitative results.

  • Document issues and improvements.

5. Deployment & Demonstration

  • Prepare a pilot deployment in a controlled greenhouse or garden.

  • Train 1–2 local users (farmers or DA staff) to operate the system.

  • Conduct a live system demonstration for panel evaluation.

6. Maintenance & Future Work

  • Draft a maintenance plan (sensor calibration, software updates, cleaning of water filters).

  • Identify limitations of the prototype and suggest future enhancements (e.g., solar power, AI-based predictive irrigation, scaling for large farms).
